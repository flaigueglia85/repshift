# RepShift Product Vision

## Product statement

RepShift is a local-first, multilingual bodybuilding and workout progression application designed to help athletes plan, execute, understand and improve their training.

The product combines four core roles:

1. **Coach** — proposes, adapts and explains training decisions.
2. **Tracker** — records workouts, performance, equipment and history.
3. **Progression engine** — identifies improvement opportunities, plateaus and fatigue patterns.
4. **Reward layer** — makes consistency and progression satisfying without becoming childish or distracting.

## Initial target user

The first release is focused on beginner and intermediate athletes training primarily for hypertrophy and bodybuilding, in commercial gyms or home gyms, from two to six sessions per week.

Advanced athletes may use the app, but the first progression rules will prioritize clarity, safety and explainability over highly specialized competition preparation.

## Product principles

### Offline-first

All essential features must work without an account, connection or subscription.

Core data is stored locally on the device:

- athlete profile;
- equipment inventory;
- plate inventory;
- training plans;
- workout sessions;
- sets and performance history;
- progression recommendations;
- preferences and language settings;
- achievements and reward state.

### Cloud-ready

Local-first must not mean local-only forever.

Every user-owned entity must be designed from the beginning so it can later be synchronized with a remote account and global platform.

Future capabilities may include:

- encrypted cloud backup;
- profile recovery across devices;
- multi-device synchronization;
- global challenges;
- leaderboards;
- shared plans;
- coach-athlete collaboration;
- community features;
- social achievements;
- optional premium services.

### Explainable intelligence

RepShift should not silently change a plan without context.

The app must clearly explain important suggestions, for example:

> You completed every set at the top of the target range with two repetitions in reserve. Increase the load next session.

The athlete keeps final control over meaningful changes.

### Useful simplicity

The application must remain readable during real training conditions:

- quick inputs;
- large touch targets;
- strong hierarchy;
- minimal cognitive load;
- clear workout state;
- useful data rather than decorative analytics.

### Motivating, not manipulative

Gamification should reinforce healthy behaviors:

- consistency;
- progression;
- honest effort reporting;
- recovery awareness;
- returning after interruptions;
- balanced training;
- completion of meaningful cycles.

The app must avoid punishing users for rest days or breaking streaks in a demotivating way.

## Core product loop

```text
Athlete setup
→ Available equipment
→ Guided plan creation
→ Adaptive daily workout
→ Fast performance logging
→ Progression analysis
→ Plan adjustment
→ Clear feedback and rewards
```

## Initial release scope

The first public version should include:

- local athlete profile;
- English and Italian localization;
- extensible localization dictionaries;
- equipment and machine setup;
- plate and barbell inventory;
- load calculator;
- curated exercise library;
- guided plan creation;
- stable and rotating exercise slots;
- live workout logging;
- workout history;
- basic adaptive workout duration;
- initial progression engine;
- weekly muscle volume overview;
- first reward and milestone system;
- local export and backup.

## Deferred capabilities

The following are intentionally deferred until the local core is proven:

- mandatory accounts;
- cloud synchronization;
- social feeds;
- public profiles;
- global challenges;
- wearable integrations;
- nutrition tracking;
- generative AI coaching;
- coach marketplace;
- subscription systems.

## Visual direction

RepShift should feel:

- modern;
- energetic;
- precise;
- premium without visual clutter;
- muscular without resembling a supplement brand;
- satisfying through restrained transitions and feedback animations.

The interface should use motion to confirm actions and progress, never to slow down workout logging.

# Visione del prodotto — Italiano

RepShift è un'applicazione multilingua e local-first dedicata principalmente al bodybuilding e alla progressione nell'allenamento.

L'app deve agire contemporaneamente come coach, diario, motore di progressione e sistema premiante.

Tutte le funzioni essenziali devono essere disponibili senza account e senza connessione. Tuttavia, profilo, storico e progressi saranno strutturati fin dall'inizio per poter essere associati in futuro a un account remoto e sincronizzati con un'infrastruttura cloud.

La futura piattaforma potrà includere backup, sincronizzazione tra dispositivi, challenge globali, classifiche, condivisione dei piani e collaborazione tra coach e atleta senza richiedere una riscrittura del modello locale.
